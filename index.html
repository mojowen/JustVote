If you need to change your address or affiliate with a political party, you can do so online or by mail. To have a ballot mailed to you, changes must be made online or the form received by the county clerk by Monday June 16th. After that date, voters need to visit a Voter Service & Polling Center to get a ballot.

The deadline for voters to change from one political party to another to vote in the primary election was Monday May 23.
